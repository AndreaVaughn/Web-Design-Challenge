Latitude vs. Cloudiness

The cloudiness does not seem to be affected by latitude. The cloudiness at 100 seems to be centeralized between -20 and 40 latitude. After 40 latitude the cloudiness drops to about 90. There seems to be an even distrabution of cloudiness between -40 and 60.
