Latitude vs. Temp

The weather becomes increasingly warmer as you get closer to the equator. Between 60 and 80 the temperature is the lowest. The higest tempurature seems to be in the -40 to -20 range. There does seem to be a correlation between the latitude and temperature as there is a curve shape in the scatter plot where a lot of data points are bunched together.
